# Quiz Game V2 - South African Edition 🇿🇦

An enhanced version of the Quiz Game with South African content!

## Features

✨ **Multiple Categories**
- 🎭 South African Culture
- 📚 SA History  
- 🍲 Traditional Food
- 🚗 SA Car Brands

🎮 **Game Features**
- Multiple difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard)
- Three question types: Multiple Choice, True/False, Text Input
- Time-based challenges with countdown timer
- Real-time scoring system
- Score tracking and leaderboard by category
- Category-specific leaderboards
- LocalStorage for persistent scores

## Question Categories

### 🎭 South African Culture (10 Questions)
Learn about Ubuntu, traditional languages, music genres like Amapiano, beadwork traditions, and more!

### 📚 SA History (10 Questions)
Test your knowledge on apartheid, Nelson Mandela, Robben Island, the Truth & Reconciliation Commission, and more.

### 🍲 Traditional Food (10 Questions)
Explore South African cuisine including Bunny Chow, Biltong, Pap, Bobotie, Braai, and more!

### 🚗 SA Car Brands (10 Questions)
Learn about South African automotive industry, major manufacturers, and local brands.

## How to Play

1. **Select a Category**: Choose from Culture, History, Food, or Car Brands
2. **Choose Difficulty**: 
   - 🟢 Easy: 10 seconds per question (1x points)
   - 🟡 Medium: 7 seconds per question (1.5x points)
   - 🔴 Hard: 5 seconds per question (2x points)
3. **Answer Questions**: 
   - Multiple choice: Click the correct answer
   - True/False: Click True or False
   - Text Input: Type your answer and press Enter
4. **View Results**: See your score and correct answers percentage
5. **Save Score**: Enter your name to save and view on the leaderboard

## Scoring System

- **Base Points**: 10 points per correct answer
- **Difficulty Multiplier**:
  - Easy: 1x = 10 points
  - Medium: 1.5x = 15 points
  - Hard: 2x = 20 points
- **Time Bonus**: Answer quickly for higher scores!
